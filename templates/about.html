About ExamMonitor

Revolutionizing academic integrity through advanced computer vision technology

Our Mission

To create a fair and transparent examination environment by leveraging cutting-edge AI technology to detect and prevent academic dishonesty, ensuring equal opportunities for all students.

What We Do

Our system uses real-time computer vision algorithms to monitor exam sessions and identify suspicious behaviors such as head movement, eye gaze patterns, prohibited objects, and unusual activities that may indicate cheating attempts.

How It Works

Using advanced machine learning models and computer vision techniques, our system analyzes video feeds in real-time to detect multiple cheating behaviors simultaneously, providing instant alerts and comprehensive reports.
